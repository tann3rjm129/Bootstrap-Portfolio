About Me

Hello, my name is Tanner McClain and I’m an aspiring full-stack developer. Though I do not have an extensive coding background, I have yet to back down from a challenge and have worked against far tougher odds weighed against me. In my educational career, I maintained a 3.4+ GPA at Sacramento State earning a BA in Government with minors in Engineering and Mathematics while working a physically demanding job in the construction field. I’ve worked for non-profits, served my community and participated in political activism. After working for a political entity for a year, I returned to the construction field to take a break from the 24-hr cycle of crazy…and this was even before Donald Trump (Ha!). I needed to maintain a job for all practical reasons, but have definitely wanted to find another field to venture into that promotes and pushes the boundaries of my abilities more than my current position.

Now on the fun side of things, I try to enjoy my limited time when I’m not working or in school. I’m a Netflix and streaming junkie, literally watched the office seasons 1-9 10 times. When I’m not trying to zone out in my bean bag (yes I own a bean bag, its huge) I’m listening to music, researching political developments, watching video commentary, the news and doing some creative writing. I still manage to be able to find time to work out frequently and go out to the bars with friends every once in a while. I’ve got a killer sense of humor, like long walks on the beach and love Chilis (the restaurant). If you want to know more, just ask!
